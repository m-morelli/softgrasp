ctraj

Cartesian trajectory between two points

tc = ctraj(T0, T1, s) as above but the elements of s specify the fractional distance along the path, and these values are in the range [0 1]. The Cartesian trajectory is a 4x4xN matrix, with transform T(:,:,i) corresponding to s(i).
